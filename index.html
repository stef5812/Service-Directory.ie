Services for Seniors

Our interactive map for seniors is a valuable resource that includes pinpointed locations for essential services catering to the needs of older individuals.

This user-friendly tool allows seniors themselves or their families to easily locate vital services such as medical facilities, police stations, Meals on Wheels distribution centers, and retirement homes in their local area.

By providing a visual representation of these services on a map, individuals can efficiently access the support and amenities most relevant to their specific needs, all within proximity to their current location.

Whether seeking medical assistance, safety and security, nutritious meals, or comfortable retirement living, our map empowers seniors to make informed decisions and enhances their quality of life.

Tourist Attractions

Our traveler's map is your trusted companion for exploring specific areas, offering a wealth of information curated by local residents who know their region best.

This dynamic map is a one-stop destination for travelers seeking accommodation options, from cozy inns to luxurious hotels, as well as an array of delectable dining establishments where you can savor local cuisine.

Discover hidden gems and must-visit spots recommended by the people who call the area home, including breathtaking beauty spots, historic landmarks, and unique attractions.

For your peace of mind, the map also pinpoints essential services such as pharmacies and police stations, ensuring you're well-prepared for any situation..

About

Where we came from


1980-Present

Our Humble Beginnings

Our story begins with two school friends from the UK who, despite the miles that separated them, remained connected over the years. Eventually, fate brought them back together, and they decided to embark on a new adventure. Their journey wasn't complete until Nataliya, hailing from Russia, joined the team through shared interests. It's these unique connections and shared experiences that laid the foundation for Services-Directory.ie!

May 2023

An idea is Born

The spark that ignited our startup's mission came from Stefan Dodds' collaboration with ALONE, an NGO dedicated to assisting the elderly. During this time, Stefan recognized the pressing need for a comprehensive services directory tailored to the needs of seniors. This realization inspired the birth of an innovative idea that would soon evolve into a dynamic platform, filling a critical gap in the market!

September 2023

Transition to Full Service

Our team's diverse skills and backgrounds converged during an exciting transition phase. Stefan's 25 years of software development expertise, Andrew's two decades in business network solutions, and Nataliya's 20 years of design experience converged to form a powerhouse of creativity, innovation, and technical prowess. This transition marked the beginning of our journey to provide full-service solutions to our users!

Ahead of us...

Phase Two Expansion

Today, Services-Directory.ie stands as a startup on the brink of phase two expansion. We've garnered attention and support from individuals and organizations across various sectors, including the medical and HSE community, women's refuge centers, and government agencies. As our services gain traction, we're branching into new territories, recently forming alliances within the tourism industry. This phase represents our commitment to meet the evolving needs of our diverse user base while continuing to make a positive impact on communities and industries alike!
